# Early Access Program (EAP)

This project is in its very early stages. The EAP helps us cover AI service costs and continue development.

## Benefits

- **Unlimited AI Usage**
- **Early Access** to new features
- **Early Feedback & Testing**

## Cost

- 0.5 SOL
- Supports development and AI costs

## Alternative

Our platform is fully open-source. If you prefer not to join the EAP, you can:

- Deploy your own instance
- Host it on your infrastructure
- Manage your own API keys
