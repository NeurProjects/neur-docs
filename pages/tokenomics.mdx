### Circulating Supply (90%)
The majority of tokens are fairly launched and available to the community from day one, ensuring wide distribution and decentralized ownership. This approach promotes a fair and inclusive ecosystem where everyone has the opportunity to participate.

### Community & Open Source (7%)
Reserved for:
- Open-source project contributions
- Developer grants and bounties
- Community-driven initiatives
- Integration partnerships
- Technical infrastructure improvements

### Team (3%)
Allocated for the core team developing the Neur framework. To ensure long-term alignment and prevent market disruption:
- Team allocations are locked in escrow
- Tokens vest linearly over a 1-year period
- Smart contract-enforced vesting schedule

## Vesting Schedule

To maintain price stability and ensure long-term commitment:

### Community & Open Source Fund (7%)
- 12-month linear vesting schedule
- Fund allocation and usage determined by community governance
- Distribution through transparent proposal systems
- All vesting mechanisms enforced by smart contracts
